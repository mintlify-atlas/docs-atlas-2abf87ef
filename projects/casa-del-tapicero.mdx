## Overview

This project involved developing a digital presence for a family business with decades of experience in upholstery, seeking to expand their reach and showcase their work and products to potential new customers.

### The Client and Their Need

La Casa del Tapicero is a workshop with over 30 years of experience in restoration, creation, and sale of upholstery supplies. Despite their excellent local reputation, they lacked a digital presence to showcase their products and attract customers beyond their immediate area.

## Implemented Solution

I developed a complete website that combines elegance with functionality, highlighting the artisanal quality of the work while providing an intuitive user experience for potential customers.

## Development Process

The project was carried out following these stages:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery & Research">
    Interviews with owners to understand their vision and needs, followed by market research and competitive analysis.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Design Phase">
    Design of wireframes and visual mockups to establish the site structure and aesthetic.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Development">
    Development of the site with modern technologies ensuring performance and scalability.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Photography">
    Professional photography session to capture the workshop and finished works.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="SEO & Setup">
    Implementation of local SEO and configuration of Google My Business for increased visibility.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Training">
    Training for owners on basic content updates to maintain the site.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Business Impact

<Info>
  After the website launch, La Casa del Tapicero experienced a significant increase in inquiries and quote requests, expanding their clientele beyond their traditional area of influence.
</Info>

The product gallery has become a valuable tool for showcasing the level of finish and craftsmanship, helping potential customers visualize the quality of work before physically visiting the workshop.
