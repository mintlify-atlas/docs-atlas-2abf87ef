## Overview

Encontrar Mascotas is a web application developed to address a common community problem: lost pets unable to reunite with their families due to the lack of a centralized and efficient system for reporting and searching.

### The Problem

Losing a pet is a distressing experience for any family. Traditional methods like street posters or general social media posts have limited reach and are not optimized for this specific purpose.

### The Solution

A dedicated web application exclusively for connecting people who have lost their pets with those who have found animals, using modern technologies to maximize the chances of successful reunions.
