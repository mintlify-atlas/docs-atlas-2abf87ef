## Overview

This project involved creating a complete digital presence for a local gym, offering a platform where potential customers can learn about the facilities, services, and values of the business.

<Card title="Visit Website" icon="link" href="https://gimnasiolevron.com.ar/">

## Project Objectives

The client needed a digital platform that would:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Showcase Facilities" icon="building">
    Present their facilities and equipment in an attractive manner.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Display Classes" icon="calendar">
    Show information about different classes and available activities.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Pricing Information" icon="dollar-sign">
    Help potential customers learn about plans and pricing.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Direct Contact" icon="phone">
    Provide a means of direct contact with the gym.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Implemented Solution

I developed a fully responsive website that meets all stated objectives, with a modern and dynamic design that reflects the energy and professionalism of the gym.
